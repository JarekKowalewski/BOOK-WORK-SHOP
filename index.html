About BOOK-WORK-SHOP

What is the BOOK-WORK-SHOP?
A bookstore?
A reading room? A writing or bookbinding workshop?
Or an original-looking coffe shop?
All of the above and even more.

BOOK-WORK-SHOP is a place with a unique atmosphere, created by book lovers for book lovers. It is also a community, fascinated by reading and creating.

Dear visitor, come inside, take a look around and fall in love with a "bookstore" you haven't seen before!

Are you curious about how a book is made? Would you like to try your hand at writing or learn the art of bookbinding?

Take advantage of our workshops - create a captivating novel or a moving poetry, bind it immortalize your own creation!

What we offer?

  1. Books! What more do we need?

  2. The wonderful atmosphere of the intimate reading room

  3. A cup of delicious, aromatic coffee? Of course!

  4. Writing workshop, because everyone has a story to write

  5. Bookbinding workshop, because every story deserves a proper binding

  6. Above all, adventure!
